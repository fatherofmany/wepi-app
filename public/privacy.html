Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

WePi ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect from you and how we use it when you interact with our app within the Pi Network ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect

  • Pi username and public wallet address via Pi SDK
  • Transaction metadata related to Pi payments

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to enable core features like sending/receiving Pi, managing your wallet, and customizing your user experience. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data is securely stored using encrypted databases. Pi authentication is handled via Pi Network’s secure SDK and payment flow.

4. Your Consent

By using the WePi app via Pi Browser, you agree to this privacy policy.
